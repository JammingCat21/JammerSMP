# Mob Bounties
Every 30 minutes, a random mob is wanted in a random biome. 

First player to kill, gets the rewards. 

## Rewards
You receive one high-end reward and 3-9 of a resource reward. 

## Mobs
- Chicken
- Cow
- Creeper
- Pig
- Sheep
- Skeleton
- Spider
- Zombie

## Biomes
- Forest
- Plains
- Desert
- Badlands
- Birch Forest
- Taiga
- Dark Forest
- Pale Forest
- Snowy Plains
- Snowy Taiga

## High End Rewards
You will receieve one of the following:
- Creeper Spawn Egg
- Pig Spawn Egg
- Cow Spawn Egg
- Sheep Spawn Egg
- Chicken Spawn Egg
- Witch Spawn Egg
- Zombie Spawn Egg
- Skeleton Spawn Egg
- Diamond Armor Piece
- Chainmail Armor Piece
- Diamond Tool (Excluding Hoe)
- Trial Key
- Ominous Trial Key
- Totem of Undying
- Shulker Box
- Netherite Ingot
- Block of Gold/Iron/Diamond/Emerald

## Resource Reward
You will receive 3-9 of the following:
- Gold Ingot
- Iron Ingot
- Diamond
- Emerald
- EXP Bottle
- Enchanted Golden Apple
- Phantom Membrane
- Slime Ball

## Preventive Measures
Metas are fine but instantaneous methods are not allowed. 
- Spawner Mobs do not count.
- Spawn Egg Mobs do not count.
